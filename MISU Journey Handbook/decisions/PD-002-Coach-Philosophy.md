\# Background



为什么 MISU Journey 需要 Coach？



Coach 在 Journey 中扮演什么角色？



\---



\# Core Philosophy



Coach exists to:



\- Encourage consistency

\- Build relationships

\- Celebrate growth

\- Guide behaviour

\- Increase customer success



Coach does NOT exist to:



\- Monitor customers

\- Judge customers

\- Police behaviour

\- Replace customer responsibility



\---



\# Product Principles



\## 1. Relationship Before Management



Coach is building relationships,

not completing tasks.



\---



\## 2. Celebrate Before Support



Every day starts with celebrating success,

then supporting customers who need help.



\---



\## 3. Support Behaviour, Not Results



The system recognises behaviours.



Examples:



\- Completed Body Progress

\- Morning Check-in

\- Learning

\- Healthy eating

\- Consistency



Instead of:



\- Lost 2kg

\- Looks slimmer

\- Perfect customer



\---



\## 4. Journey Consistency, Not Product Compliance



Journey Consistency measures whether

customers are still participating in their Journey.



It does NOT measure whether

customers strictly follow one fixed product schedule.



Each Coach may recommend different MISU usage strategies.



Therefore the system evaluates participation,

not exact sachet consumption.



\---



\## 5. AI Assists, Coach Decides



AI helps organise information.



AI never replaces coaching.



Official Coaching Script always has higher priority than AI.



\---



\## 6. Customer Context Before Contact



Before contacting a customer,

the Coach should already know:



\- Journey Stage

\- Recent Progress

\- Current Challenges

\- Support Signals



The system prepares context.



The Coach builds the relationship.



\---



\## 7. One Daily Workspace



Coach should not browse multiple pages.



The system answers one question:



Who should I celebrate today?



Who needs my support today?



\---



\## 8. Human Communication



Every coaching interaction should feel:



\- Personal

\- Warm

\- Encouraging



Never robotic.



Never task-oriented.



\---



\# Future Direction



Future Coach features may include:



\- Coaching Playbook

\- Coaching Script CMS

\- AI Draft Assistant

\- Coach Intelligence

\- Team Coaching Insights

\- Success Timeline



All future Coach features must continue following this philosophy.



\---



\# Final Principle



Great coaching is not measured by

how many messages are sent.



It is measured by

how many customers continue their Journey.



The system guides the Coach. The Coach guides the customer. Together, they build lasting habits
