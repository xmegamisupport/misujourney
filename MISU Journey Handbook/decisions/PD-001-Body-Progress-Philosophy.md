\# Background



Body Progress is not designed to judge a customer's body.



Its purpose is to help customers record their health journey, stay motivated, and create a meaningful timeline of their personal transformation.



The system should always encourage healthy habits instead of creating pressure or disappointment.



\---



\# Core Philosophy



Body Progress exists to:



\- Record growth.

\- Encourage consistency.

\- Build confidence.

\- Help Coaches provide better support.

\- Preserve meaningful customer milestones.



Body Progress does NOT exist to:



\- Judge appearance.

\- Compare customers.

\- Evaluate beauty.

\- Pressure customers.

\- Replace Coach encouragement.



\---



\# Product Principles



\## 1. Celebrate Behaviour, Not Results



The system celebrates effort.



Examples:



\- Completed Body Progress

\- Completed Morning Check-in

\- Consecutive Check-in

\- Completed Learning

\- Healthy Habit Completion



The system should never evaluate:



\- Weight loss

\- Body shape improvement

\- Visual appearance

\- Whether the customer changed "enough"



Results should always be recognised by Coaches, not automatically judged by AI.



\---



\## 2. Record Growth, Don't Judge Growth



Body Progress is a personal history.



Every submitted record becomes part of the customer's journey.



Historical records cannot be edited or deleted.



Customers may only replace photos before the current submission is confirmed.



Once confirmed:



History becomes permanent.



\---



\## 3. Customer Experience First



Body Progress should never become a barrier.



Customers may postpone the first recording.



Journey can start immediately.



The system encourages completion but never blocks the customer's progress.



\---



\## 4. Coach Supports, AI Assists



AI provides:



\- reminders

\- organisation

\- encouragement

\- automation



Coach provides:



\- encouragement

\- observation

\- personalised feedback

\- emotional support



AI should never replace Coach relationships.



\---



\## 5. Privacy First



Progress photos belong to customers.



Photos are private.



Only:



\- Customer

\- Assigned Coach

\- Admin



may access them.



No photos may be used for marketing without explicit customer permission.



Media authorisation is completely independent from Body Progress.



\---



\## 6. Long-term Habit Building



The purpose of Body Progress is to build long-term healthy habits.



It is not designed for short-term weight loss.



Customers should always feel:



"I am becoming healthier."



instead of



"I am being judged."



\---



\# Future Direction



Future modules may build upon this philosophy:



\- Journey Star

\- Lifestyle Mode

\- Coach Intelligence

\- Success Gallery

\- Media Authorisation

\- AI Growth Timeline



All future features must continue following this philosophy.



\---



\# Final Principle



MISU Journey is designed to celebrate consistency,

not perfection.



Healthy habits create lasting results.



The system exists to support that journey.
