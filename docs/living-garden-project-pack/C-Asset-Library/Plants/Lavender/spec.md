# Lavender

**Asset Name:** Lavender Plant

**Category:** Plants

**Purpose:** The first purple focal accent — a calm, elegant note of colour in a
mostly green scene.

**Unlock Stage:** Day 13

## Visual Description

A graceful lavender plant with several dusty-purple flower spikes and narrow
muted-green leaves. Softly detailed, elegant and calm, with a clear purple
accent.

## Color Palette

- Flower spikes: dusty lavender purple
- Leaves / stems: narrow muted green, sage green

## Shape Language

Tall slender upright spikes of varying heights, narrow leaves, gentle natural
sway — asymmetrical, never a uniform fan.

## Lighting

Warm golden morning light from the upper left; soft highlights along the spikes;
gentle natural shadow; no harsh black shadow.

## Scale

Small-to-medium; taller than it is wide. A vertical accent among rounder plants.

## Export

- Transparent PNG
- High resolution
- Centered composition with safe transparent padding
