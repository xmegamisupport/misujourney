\## Subject



Create the fully grown Ancient Tree shown in the official Living Garden Master Artwork.



The tree should match the Master Artwork in:



\- overall silhouette

\- proportions

\- trunk shape

\- canopy shape

\- painting style

\- perspective

\- lighting



Do not redesign the tree.



Do not invent a new tree.



The objective is to isolate the existing Ancient Tree from the Master Artwork as a production-ready asset.
