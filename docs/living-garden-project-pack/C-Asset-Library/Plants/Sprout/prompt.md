Permanent generation prompt for Hero Asset 001. Use for every iteration until the

## Subject Prompt

A single tiny newborn plant sprout with exactly two rounded cotyledon leaves in a
soft open V-shape on one slender, gently curved stem rising from soil. Hand-painted
storybook watercolour illustration; layered translucent washes with soft internal
gradients, subtle pigment granulation, and a gentle bloom where colour pools at the
base. Fresh leaf-green deepening to cool olive-moss at the base, with warm
yellow-green sunlit highlights and softly translucent leaf tips. Natural asymmetry:
one leaf slightly larger and higher, reaching toward the light; the stem leaning a
few degrees toward the upper-left. Soft, slightly irregular paint-defined edges —
no outline. Warm golden morning light from the upper-left, gentle soft shadow to
the lower-right, and a small soft diffuse contact shadow directly beneath the base.
Rooted at the bottom-centre of the frame, growing upward, with open empty space
above and around it. Calm, hopeful, tender, premium, timeless picture-book quality.
Transparent background, isolated single object.

## Negative Prompt

no outline, no black keyline, no vector look, no flat single-colour fill, no
cartoon, no chibi, no glossy highlight dot, no 3D, no plastic, no bevel, no drop
shadow, no sticker edge, no white halo, no die-cut border, no neon or lime green,
no symmetry, no extra leaves, no second sprout, no flower, no pot, no background, no
grey background bleed, no box, no frame, no text, no watermark, no photorealism, no
anime.

## Technical

Transparent PNG · single subject · light upper-left · base at bottom-centre · trim
to content + ~3% uniform transparent padding · master ≥1024px, production ~512px.

## Generation Notes

This asset is the visual benchmark for every future plant asset — and the quality
bar for the whole Living Garden. Judge every other asset by whether it belongs
beside this sprout.
