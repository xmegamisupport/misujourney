## Day 35 Rule

For v1, Day 35 does not need a complicated rainbow, house, animation or story ending.

Its reward is the complete, rich garden composition.

A special final asset can be added later only if Founder Preview shows that Day 35 needs a stronger visual conclusion.

## Reuse Rule

Approved assets may be reused by adjusting:

- x and y position
- scale
- horizontal flip
- small rotation
- z-index

Do not overuse the same asset so frequently that repetition becomes obvious.
