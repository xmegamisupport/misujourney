# Living Garden Founder Review Checklist

Use Founder Preview to review at least:

- Day 1
- Day 3
- Day 5
- Day 7
- Day 10
- Day 14
- Day 18
- Day 21
- Day 25
- Day 30
- Day 35

## Daily Change

- Can I notice what changed without being told?
- Is the new element visible but not oversized?
- Does the new element feel naturally placed?
- Is only one main change competing for attention?
- Does the scene still feel calm?

## Growth Progression

- Does Day 7 feel richer than Day 1?
- Does Day 14 feel richer than Day 7?
- Does Day 21 feel richer than Day 14?
- Does Day 30 feel substantially richer than Day 21?
- Does Day 35 feel complete without becoming crowded?

## Visual Quality

- Are the colors rich enough?
- Is the garden too pale?
- Is any asset too saturated?
- Do all assets look like the same illustrator created them?
- Are any assets too small to notice on mobile?
- Are there obvious repeated copies?
- Does the garden retain breathing space?
- Is greenery still the dominant visual foundation?

## Product Simplicity

- Is there unnecessary text?
- Is there an unnecessary popup?
- Is there an unnecessary animation?
- Is the user allowed to discover the change naturally?
- Does the experience remain simple?

## Review Result

Record each review as:

- Approved
- Needs repositioning
- Needs scaling
- Needs art revision
- Needs progression change
