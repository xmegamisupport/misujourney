## Deferred Assets

Do not produce these during the first calibration batch:

- full cottage
- large bridge
- waterfall
- full stream
- full-scene background
- complete flower field
- rainbow overlay
- sunlight effect
- flying-petal effect

These assets require separate scene-level or effect-level specifications.
